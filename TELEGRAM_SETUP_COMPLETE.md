## 🛍️ How to Place Your First Test Order

### Step 1: Browse Products
1. Visit: http://localhost:5175/shop
2. Browse through 8 available products
3. Click **"QUICK VIEW"** on any product

### Step 2: Customize Product
1. Select **Size** (S, M, L, XL, XXL, etc.)
2. Select **Color** (click color swatch)
3. Adjust **Quantity** (+/- buttons)
4. Click **"ADD TO CART"**

### Step 3: Review Cart
1. Click **cart icon** in navbar
2. Verify item appears in cart drawer
3. Check quantity and price
4. Adjust if needed

### Step 4: Checkout
1. Click **"CHECKOUT"** button
2. Fill in customer information:
   - Full Name
   - Email Address
   - Phone Number
   - Street Address
   - City
   - Postal Code
   - Country
3. Add optional notes
4. Click **"COMPLETE ORDER"**

### Step 5: Success!
1. See success confirmation screen
2. Cart automatically clears
3. **Check your Telegram Group "kec"**
4. You should see order notification within 1-2 seconds! ⚡
